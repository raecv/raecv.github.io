I am a (almost) newly-minted PhD from Stanford Genetics!

I work on customizing high-throughput genetic screens to explore new sides of gene regulation. I am currently wrapping up my dissertation, which focuses on improving functional predictions of human variants and advancing synthetic design of transcriptional effectors, using large-scale data and deep learning.

I'm guided by a genuine enthusiasm for team science, people-driven progress, open communication, and diversity in acedemia. I'm currently looking for science writing or editing jobs, with a start date as soon as, well, very soon.
